Hi there, my name is Valentina. This site is a glimpse into my awesome world, and what I think about it. I may not be 2 years old yet, but I've already experienced a lot of great things thanks to my amazing parents, and my incredible family and friends. We live in a crazy world, but it's all pretty simple to me. Hopefully I can convince you of that so that you can appreciate all the little things that make our world great, funny, kind, loud, messy, fast, and fun!
